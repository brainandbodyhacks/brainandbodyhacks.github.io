# Nizza-Salad
A quick and filling summer-dish.

# Ingredients
For 1 serving:
- Lettuce
- Carrot, Cucumber
- Red onion
- 1 small can tuna
- Vinegar, Olive oil
- Hard boiled egg
- Optional: Feta, Sprouts, cabbage

# Steps
- Mix all ingredients
- Salt and pepper

# Healthy because
 A great mix of protein, veggies and fibre.
